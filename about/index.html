Fueled by Faith Blogs


"If you wish to have Eternal Life, keep my commandments." -Matthew 19:17

    Welcome to my blog, Fueled by Faith. My name is Ryan William Moore and I grew up in small-town, Western Pennsylvania. I can't lie, I haven't been a Christian very long - you can read my testimony for more details - but I've felt lead to journaling during sermons. Sermon-journaling lead to thought journaling, which lead to prayer journaling, which now leads us here: online jounraling (blogging). I wanted to place to collect my thoughts, prayers, lessons, devotionals, and other significant things that I can share with others. On top of that, I want to collaborate with other believers so that they can contribute to sharing the Word of God.
